## 2. Description (Detailed)

A straightforward, open-source currency converter that respects your privacy.

**What it does**
*   **Auto-Conversion**: Automatically detects and converts prices on web pages.
*   **Interactive Mode**: Select any price and a popup appears with the converted value.
*   **Smart Detection**: Handles varied formats, including text mentions, and ambiguous symbols (like '$').

**Why this extension?**
*   **100% Open Source**: You can verify the code yourself. No hidden scripts.
*   **Privacy First**: No analytics. No tracking. Your data stays on your device.
*   **Reliable**: Uses daily rates from the European Central Bank.
*   **No Bloat**: No ads, no paywalls, no unnecessary permissions.

It just works.
